Data Collection - How to Add Your Own Trees!


Know of a fruit tree in your area? Want to share that precious yield with everyone around you?


This form will allow you to set the location and choose the plant type (Tree/shrub).

One tree may be submitted at a time.


Are you currently at the tree you wish to add?

- Click the location icon to the right of the search bar. Your coordinates will automatically populate!


Are you somehwere else?

- Search for an address in the search bar

- Pan and zoom to locate your Tree

- Click the location on the map to set your point

Web Application - Accessing the Data!


Next Step is to navigate to the Web Application in your broswer: Click Here

First, a disclaimer must be acknowledged. Incorrect identification of edible plants poses a huge risk. The data on this site is provided by the public and may be used at your own risk.


Once you have acknowledged this disclaimer, you have access to the data!

Functionaility - Viewing the Data!


Now you are ready to look at the trees in your area!

You can pan and zoom on this map to view your area of choice. It is centred on Kawartha Lakes.

Clicking a point will display a pop-up, which identifies the plant type!

If you don't see your points, our system updates every 30 minutes, so be patient!

Rainviewer - Weather Radar for Harvest!


The next steps are to get out there and harvest some food!

An important part of harvesting fruits is making sure you do it in ideal conditions.

You can pan and zoom on this map to view your area of choice. It is centred on Kawartha Lakes.


How to use the Rainviewer

In the bottom left corner of the application, click the weather icon to view current weather conditions.

Adjust the opacity and time slide bars to view the rain conditions.
